Taco Trivia

When did Tacos first appear in United States?

Tacos first appeared in the United States around the early 1900s when Mexican migrant workers brought them to the Southwest for work in mines and on railroads. The first recorded mention of tacos in a U.S. newspaper was in 1905.
(source:Twisted Taco)
