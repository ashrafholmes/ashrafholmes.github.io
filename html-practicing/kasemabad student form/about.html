Kasemabad Siddikia Kamil Madrasha

Kasemabad Siddikia Kamil Madrasah is the official name of the academic institute, as registered with the education authorities. The native or alternative name of the institute is কাসেমবাদ সিদ্দিকিয়া কামিল মাদ্রাসা, often used in local Bengali language contexts. The unique Educational Institute Identification Number (EIIN) assigned to this institute is 100687, provided by the Directorate of Secondary and Higher Education. The institute was established on 08 December, 1932, marking the beginning of its educational journey. The institute is officially recognized by the relevant authorities as Recognized. The institute received official recognition on 01 July, 1978. It is recognized at the Kamil, indicating the level or grade of approval.
